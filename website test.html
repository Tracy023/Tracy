Rocky: A Serif Font


Type Designer: Matthew Carter

Carter & Cone publishes the designs of Matthew Carter, including Bell Centennial, Big Caslon, Miller, and Roster, and custom type for clients like the Museum of Modern Art, The New York Times, Sports Illustrated, and Yale University. Carter also worked with Microsoft on a series of screen fonts designed to maximize the legibility of type on computer monitors. Named a Royal Designer for Industry in the UK, Carter received the Lifetime Achievement Award from the Smithsonian National Design Awards in 2011.


Also created other fonts such as: Big Caslon, Big Moore, Georgia Pro, Mantinia, Miller Banner, Miller Display, Miller Headline, Miller Text, Roster, Sophia, Stilson, and Verdana Pro.



It belongs to the humanist serif category, making it an ideal choice for a wide range of applications. Its elegant and classic letterforms are well-suited for body text, headings, and other design elements.
